**Student Management Hub** is a modern Student Information System (SIS) designed to bridge the communication gap between educators and students. It provides a centralized platform for managing academic records, tracking attendance, and monitoring student progress in real-time.

---

##  Key Features

###  For Teachers
*   **Grade Management:** Easily input, update, and manage grades for exams, assignments, and projects.
*   **Attendance Tracking:** Digital roll-call system to record daily or course-specific attendance.
*   **Class Analytics:** View overall class performance and identify students who may need extra support.

###  For Students
*   **Grade Portal:** Instant access to published grades and academic feedback.
*   **Attendance Overview:** Stay informed about total attendance percentages and missed classes.
*   **Progress Dashboard:** A personalized view of academic standing and upcoming deadlines.
